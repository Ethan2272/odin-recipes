Mango Lassi

Many famous texts speak of a nectar of the gods known as Ambrosia. Maybe they would move on if they just tried this instead.

Ingredients

  • 2 (15.25 ounce) cans mango pulp, or mango slices with juice
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Blend mango pulp, yogurt, milk, and ice in a blender until smooth
  2. Pour into tall glasses and serve.
